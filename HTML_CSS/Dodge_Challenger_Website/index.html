Choose your engine

3.6 litre V6

305 HP | 268 LB-FT OF TORQUE
The standard 3.6L Pentastar® V6 engine combined with the standard TorqueFlite® eight-speed automatic transmission delivers an impressive 303 horsepower. Standard on SXT and GT with RWD and available with AWD. Our philosophy is there are no limits to performance. If there’s a line, the Dodge Brand is obliged to cross it, and the 2021 Dodge Challenger continues to lead the pack in the race for horsepower and high performance.

5.7 litre V8

372 HP | 400 LB-FT OF TORQUE
Where efficiency meets performance, the 5.7L HEMI® V8 engine delivers up to 375 horsepower and includes fuel saver MDS technology to switch from eight cylinders to four when you want to save on fuel consumption. Standard on the Challenger R/T.

6.2 litre V8

807 HP | 707 LB-FT OF TORQUE
The Supercharged 6.2L High-Output HEMI® V8 engine in the SRT® Super Stock was built to feed the power hungry. With the highest horsepower of any production car of up to 807 horsepower and 707 pound-feet of torque, the Dodge Challenger Super Stock is the most powerful muscle car
